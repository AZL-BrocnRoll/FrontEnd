🌿 Health Self-Assessment Quiz

1. Do you eat at least 2 servings of fruits and 3 servings of vegetables daily?
2. Do you limit fast food and sugary snacks to once or twice a week?
3. Do you drink at least 6–8 glasses of water daily?
4. Do you exercise at least 150 minutes per week?
5. Do you avoid sitting too long without stretching?
6. Do you sleep 7–9 hours a night and feel rested?
7. Do you feel your stress is under control?
8. Do you use healthy ways to manage stress?
9. Do you avoid smoking and limit alcohol to healthy levels?
10. Do you limit screen time, especially before bed?
11. Do you go for routine medical or dental checkups yearly?
12. Do you take time regularly for self-care activities (e.g. hobbies, relaxation, reflection)?
